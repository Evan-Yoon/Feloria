# Feloria: A Feline Monster-Taming RPG

Feloria is a top-down, retro-style monster-taming RPG built with Phaser 3. Explore a world entirely dedicated to collecting, battling, and evolving various elemental cats. 

## Features up to Phase 9

*   **Exploration:** Wander through Starwhisk Village, Greenpaw Forest, andMosslight Path.
*   **Feline-Only Lore:** Collect up to 30 unique, purely feline creatures ranging from Forest and Water types to rare Mystics and Legendaries.
*   **Wild Battles & Capture:** Encounter wild cats in tall grass, weaken them with attacks, and capture them using Capture Crystals.
*   **Trainer Battles:** Face off against NPCs like 'Trainer Red' with specialized cat parties.
*   **Progression & Evolution:** Level up your cats, and watch them evolve when they reach specific thresholds!
*   **Economy:** Earn gold from battles to buy Healing Herbs and Capture Crystals from the Village Shop.
*   **Persistent Save/Load:** Your progress, party, collection, and defeated trainers are all saved automatically to your browser's local storage.
*   **Quests & Codex:** Track your adventures via the Quest menu and consult your Codex for detailed lore on every cat you encounter.
*   **Visual Presentation:** Immersive 16-bit style pixel art placeholder sprites, tilesets, and a unified retro UI aesthetic.

## Controls

*   **WASD or Arrow Keys**: Move your character around the overworld.
*   **Spacebar**: Interact with NPCs, read signs, or progress dialogue menus.
*   **ESC or ENTER**: Open or close the main pause menu (to access Party, Inventory, Quests, Codex, Save/Load).
*   **Mouse Click**: Select actions in battles, click through menus, or choose dialogue options.

## How to Play Locally

1. Install dependencies: `npm install`
2. Start the development server: `npm run dev`
3. Open `http://localhost:5173` (or the port defined by Vite) in your browser.

*Note: For the best experience, click "Start New Game" on your first run, enter a name, and choose your starter kitten!*
