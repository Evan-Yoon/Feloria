# Feloria: A Feline Monster-Taming RPG

Feloria is a top-down, retro-style RPG where you explore a world entirely populated by various elemental cats. Collect, train, and evolve your feline companions to restore balance to the continent.

## 🗺️ Exploration
Travel through diverse environments:
- **Starwhisk Village**: Your base of operations.
- **Greenpaw Forest**: Wild encounters and training grounds.
- **Ancient Forest & Shrine**: The mysterious heart of the world.

## 🐱 Game Flow
1. **Choose your starter**: Pick between Leafy, Fire, or Water feline friends.
2. **Collect & Battle**: Encounter wild cats in tall grass and challenge other Trainers.
3. **Evolve**: Watch your cats grow into more powerful forms as they level up.
4. **Quest**: Follow the story from simple errands to uncovering the secrets of the Ancient Shrine.

## ⌨️ Controls
- **Movement**: WASD or Arrow Keys
- **Interact**: Spacebar
- **Menu**: ESC or Enter
- **Battle**: Mouse Click selection

## 🚀 Running Locally
1. `npm install`
2. `npm run dev`
3. Visit `http://localhost:5173`

Built with **Phaser 3** and Vite.
