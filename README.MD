# Feloria: A Feline Monster-Taming RPG

Feloria is a top-down, retro-style RPG where you explore a world entirely populated by various elemental cats. Collect, train, and evolve your feline companions to restore balance to the continent.

## ✨ Latest Update: Premium UI Overhaul

The entire game has been updated with a modern **Glassmorphism** aesthetic:

- **Unified Visuals**: Semi-transparent rounded panels with glowing light-blue and gold borders.
- **Redesigned HUD**: Vibrant currency and quest tracking.
- **Stylish Menus**: Overhauled Bag, Party, Shop, and Save/Load interfaces.
- **Immersive Dialog**: Modern dialogue boxes with high-quality portrait support.

## 🐱 Features

- **100 Unique Creatures**: Collect cats across 10 elemental types (Forest, Fire, Water, Light, Dark, etc.).
- **Class System**: Discover **Starter**, **Normal**, **Rare**, **Epic**, and **Legendary** tiered creatures.
- **Deep Progression**: Level up your cats to learn powerful skills (Tier 1-3 and Legendary exclusives).
- **Party Management**: Build your dream team of 3 cats from your collection.
- **Quest System**: Follow an evolving story from Starwhisk Village to the Ancient Shrine.
- **Save System**: Multiple save slots and auto-save support.

## 🗺️ Exploration

Travel through diverse environments:

- **Starwhisk Village**: Your base of operations.
- **Greenpaw Forest**: Wild encounters and training grounds.
- **Ancient Forest & Shrine**: The mysterious heart of the world.

## ⌨️ Controls

- **Movement**: `WASD` or Arrow Keys
- **Interact**: `Spacebar` or `Enter`
- **Menu**: `ESC` or `Enter`
- **Monster Codex**: `C`
- **Quest Log**: Accessible via Menu

## 🚀 Running Locally

1. `npm install`
2. `npm run dev`
3. Visit `http://localhost:5173`

Built with **Phaser 3** and **Vite**.
