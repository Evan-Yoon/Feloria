# Feloria: A Feline Monster-Taming RPG

![Feloria Game Icon](icon.png)

Feloria is a top-down, retro-style RPG where you explore a world entirely populated by various elemental cats. Collect, train, and evolve your feline companions to restore balance to the continent.

## 🐱 Welcome to the Continent of Feloria

Step into a world where whiskers and magic collide. As a new Cat Tamer, your journey begins in the peaceful Starwhisk Village, but the path ahead is filled with mystery and danger.

### ✨ Features

- **100+ Unique Creatures**: Collect and bond with cats across 10 elemental types including Forest, Fire, Water, Light, and Dark.
- **Strategic Combat**: Build a dream team of 3 cats and master their unique skills (Tier 1-3) to overcome challenges.
- **Rarity Classes**: Discover everything from common **Starters** to legendary **Ancient Kitties**.
- **Modern HUD**: Enjoy a sleek, **Glassmorphism** UI with semi-transparent panels and glowing accents.
- **Deep Lore**: Follow the story through the Greenpaw Forest and uncover the secrets of the Ancient Shrine.

## 🗺️ Exploration

Travel through diverse environments:
- **Starwhisk Village**: Your base of operations and home of the Elder Cat.
- **Greenpaw Forest**: A lush wilderness perfect for training new recruits.
- **Ancient Shrine**: The heart of Feloria, where the most powerful legends sleep.

## ⌨️ Controls

Experience the journey with simple, intuitive controls:

- **Move**: `WASD` or Arrow Keys
- **Interact / Talk**: `Spacebar` or `Enter`
- **Open Menu**: `ESC` or `Enter` (inside game)
- **Monster Codex**: `C`

## 🌟 Quality of Life

- **Save Anywhere**: Multiple save slots and auto-save support ensure your progress is never lost.
- **Dynamic HUD**: Real-time currency and quest tracking at your fingertips.
- **Portrait Dialog**: High-quality portraits for every NPC you meet.
